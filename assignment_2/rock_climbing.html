Indoor Rock Climbing

The basics

indoor rock climbing is like climbing outdoors but better. You never have to worry about the rain, snow, the temperature outside, all you have to worry about the how much time you want to spend there. Indoor rock climbing is an inexpesive way to workout, relive stress, and meet some aweseome new people as you tackle routs together!

What to Wear

The only thing you need to climb are shoes. You dont need specialized clithing tailor made for clibming to get started. In fact, alot of the time people will wear their work clohtes since they want to work out before going home from work. They just always keep thier shoes in their car so they can go whenever they like. But do keep in mind that the shoe needs to be a sung fit on your feet and in general is about two or three times sizes smaller than what you normally wear.
